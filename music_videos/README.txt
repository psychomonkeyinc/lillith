Music Video Playlist for LILLITH
==================================================

Place the following 6 music video files in this directory:

1. beach_boys.mp4 - Beach Boys music video
2. beatles.mp4 - Beatles music video
3. jim_croce.mp4 - Jim Croce music video
4. depeche_mode.mp4 - Depeche Mode music video
5. nirvana.mp4 - Nirvana music video
6. backstreet_boys.mp4 - Backstreet Boys music video

These videos will play in a continuous loop, providing
unified audio+video input for passive learning.

The system learns by continuous exposure ('babysitting'),
not through explicit training runs.
